Plotting SDG Regional Scores

According to Sustainable Development Report 2022, for the second year in a row, the world is no longer making progress on the SDGs. This visualization explores the 2022 SDG progress scores at regional level. The world is lagging behind significantly in areas such as Gender Equality (Goal 5) and Reducing Equalities (Goal 10). Regional differences are also prominent, particularly in areas such as Responsible Consumption and Production (Goal 12) and Climate Action (Goal 13). Data was gathered from Sustainable Development Report's SDG Index Dashboard.
